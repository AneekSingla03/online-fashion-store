About StyleHub

Fashion with purpose, style with soul

We're Building the Future of Fashion

StyleHub was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: make fashion accessible, sustainable, and joyful for everyone in India.

From a small team of 4 in Chitkara University, we've grown to serve over few customers across the country, offering 15,000+ styles across women's, men's, kids', ethnic, and sports fashion.
