What can we help you with?

🔍
📦 Orders
How do I place an order?+
Simply browse our products, select your size, click "Add to Cart", and proceed to checkout. You can pay via UPI, credit/debit card, or Cash on Delivery. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.
Can I modify or cancel my order?+
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it. After that, if the order is already shipped, you'll need to follow the return process. Contact us at support@stylehub.in immediately.
How do I track my order?+
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section under your profile.
What if I receive a wrong or damaged item?+
We're sorry for the inconvenience! Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order ID and a photo of the item. We'll arrange an immediate replacement or full refund — no questions asked.
Can I place a bulk or wholesale order?+
Yes! For bulk orders (10+ items), please reach out to us at our B2B page or email business@stylehub.in. We offer special pricing and customization for corporate and wholesale orders.
🚚 Shipping & Delivery
How long does delivery take?+
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available for metro cities at an additional charge of ₹99. Delivery times may vary during peak seasons and festivals.
Is shipping free?+
Yes! Shipping is completely free on all orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a nominal shipping charge of ₹99 applies. You can always add more items to your cart to qualify for free shipping.
Do you deliver outside India?+
Currently, StyleHub delivers only within India to 500+ cities and towns. We're working on international shipping and hope to launch it soon. Stay tuned!
What if I'm not available to receive my order?+
Our delivery partner will attempt delivery up to 3 times. You can also reschedule delivery by contacting the courier directly using the tracking link. Alternatively, you can pick it up from the nearest delivery hub.
🔄 Returns & Exchanges
What is your return policy?+
We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. The item must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. Some items like innerwear and customized products are not eligible for return.
How do I initiate a return or exchange?+
Log into your account → My Orders → Select the item → Click "Return/Exchange". Fill in the reason and submit. A pickup will be scheduled within 2–3 business days. Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days after pickup.
How long does the refund take?+
Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. The amount is credited to your original payment method — UPI/bank account (2–3 days), card (5–7 days), or as StyleHub wallet credits (instant).
Can I exchange for a different size or color?+
Yes! Exchanges for different sizes or colors are free of charge, subject to availability. If the requested item is out of stock, we'll issue a full refund or store credit.
💳 Payment
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept all major payment methods: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders up to ₹10,000.
Is my payment information secure?+
Absolutely. All transactions are encrypted using 256-bit SSL technology. We never store your card details on our servers. Our payment gateway is PCI-DSS compliant and certified by leading security auditors.
Do you have EMI options?+
Yes! EMI is available on orders above ₹3,000 through select credit cards (HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Axis) for 3, 6, and 12 month tenures. No-cost EMI is available on select products during promotional periods.
My payment failed but money was deducted. What should I do?+
Don't worry! If a payment fails after being deducted, the amount is automatically reversed to your account within 5–7 business days by your bank. If not, email us at support@stylehub.in with your transaction ID and we'll resolve it immediately.
👤 Account & Profile
Do I need an account to shop?+
You can browse and add items to your cart without an account. However, to complete a purchase, you'll need to create a free account or log in. This helps us save your order history, addresses, and preferences.
How do I reset my password?+
Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a password reset link. The link is valid for 24 hours. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email.
How do I delete my account?+
We're sad to see you go! To delete your account, please contact us at support@stylehub.in with your registered email. We'll process the request within 7 business days. Note: Order history and pending refunds must be resolved before deletion.
📏 Sizing & Fit
How do I find my correct size?+
Each product page has a detailed Size Guide tab with chest, waist, and hip measurements. We recommend measuring yourself with a measuring tape and comparing with our size chart. When in doubt, size up — you can always exchange for a better fit.
Do your clothes run true to size?+
Most of our products are true to size. However, this can vary by brand and fabric type. We recommend checking customer reviews for size feedback, as many shoppers mention if items run small or large.
Do you offer plus sizes?+
Yes! We believe fashion is for everybody. We offer sizes from XS to XXXL (and up to 3XL for select categories). Use the size filter on the products page to browse items available in your size.
🛍️ Products & Quality
Are all products authentic and original?+
100% yes! We source directly from verified brands and manufacturers. Every product listed on StyleHub passes our quality check before it's made available to customers. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods.
How do I wash and care for my clothes?+
Each garment comes with a care label inside. General tips: machine wash on gentle cycle for cottons, hand wash for delicates and ethnic wear, dry-clean only for silk sarees and sherwanis. Avoid direct sunlight for colored fabrics.
How do I use a coupon or promo code?+
On the Cart page, you'll find a "Have a coupon?" section. Enter your code and click "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your order total. Only one coupon can be applied per order. Try code STYLE20 for 20% off!
